The Conservation Crisis

South Africa is home to the majority of the world's rhinos, but they are fighting a daily battle for survival. Driven by the lucrative and illegal wildlife trade, poachers are relentlessly targeting these animals for their horns, leaving a devastating impact on the ecosystem and local communities.

Through our documentary, "Long Swim To Freedom," we are capturing the frontline reality of this war. We work closely with anti-poaching units, conservationists, and locals who are dedicating their lives to protecting these animals. The images and stories we gather are essential tools in raising awareness and securing the funding needed to win this fight.
