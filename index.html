Long Swim To Freedom

A documentary expedition to protect South Africa's rhinos and save children from drowning. .

  • Discover the Mission

The Human Crisis

For many children in South Africa, the daily commute to school is a life-threatening journey. Forced to cross dangerous bodies of water without basic swimming education, these students risk drowning simply to get an education. We aim to shed light on this issue to bring vital swimming lessons and safety resources to the communities that need them most.

  • Learn More

The Conservation Crisis

The illegal wildlife trade has ignited a brutal poaching epidemic across South Africa, bringing rhinos to the brink of extinction. Due to the demand for their horns, poachers leave behind a trail of devastated ecosystems and orphaned calves. We want to document this war, raise awareness about the harsh reality and support the anti-poaching units fighting to protect these animals.

  • Learn More

The Documentary: Behind the Lens

We are on the ground in South Africa, capturing the intersection of these two critical crises. Long Swim To Freedom is more than a film; it is a movement to bring essential swimming education to vulnerable youth and vital funding to frontline anti-poaching units. See our work in action.

Where Your Support Goes

To make Long Swim To Freedom a reality and create lasting change, we need your help. Your funding directly supports our on-the-ground efforts in South Africa, from the documentary production to immediate community and conservation interventions.

Water Safety

Funding certified swimming instructors, safe pool access, and essential water safety gear for local children.

Anti-Poaching Support

Equipping frontline rangers with vital survival gear, medical supplies, and technological support to protect the rhinos.

Film Production

Covering camera gear, crew logistics, and post-production costs to bring this vital documentary to life.

Community Outreach

Working directly alongside community leaders to build sustainable educational programs and safe transport alternatives.

Logistics & Transport

Navigating the challenging South African terrain safely for both our documentary crew and the communities we assist.

Global Impact

Marketing and distribution of the finished documentary to maximize worldwide awareness and drive policy change.
